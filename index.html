we can do it

Whoever you
If you ever open this
By your light
You can keep it
By the bed for your head
And arms to weigh heavy
On moulting white clouds.

A raft on a plate
In the molten sea
Can close your house and
Quit you for it. You really
Can stop lying to yourself.

I know to suffer
Alone is not an innovation.
You know this one
Too. And to divine
Wisdom in a purl
Of blood takes art
In this open world
You know.

It takes art

And you have it.

From Mercury. Ariana Reines, Fence Books, 2011.
